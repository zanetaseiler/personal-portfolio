# Taste rubric

Score each restyled section 0–2 on every line before moving on. Aim for all 2s.
0 = fails, 1 = passable, 2 = intentional and polished.

## Layout & space
- Whitespace is generous and intentional (not cramped, not empty by accident).
- Content sits on a consistent grid / max-width; alignment is deliberate.
- Clear focal point — the eye knows where to go first.

## Typography
- Strong size contrast between display, heading, and body.
- Leading (line-height) and tracking (letter-spacing) are tuned, not default.
- No more than 2 type families; weights used with purpose.

## Color & depth
- Palette is tied to the brand, limited, and confident.
- NOT the default AI purple/indigo gradient on white.
- Depth via layered surfaces + soft, realistic shadows (not harsh default box-shadow).

## Detail
- Radii, borders, and states (hover, focus, active, disabled) are all handled.
- Focus states are visible and accessible.
- Imagery/icons feel custom and cohesive, not random stock.

## Motion (after Step 5)
- Easing is spring/expo, never linear.
- Things enter with stagger and rhythm, not all at once.
- Motion has a clear purpose (guide attention / show relationship), not decoration for its own sake.
- Everything degrades gracefully with reduced motion.

**Gate:** if any section scores below 1 on any line, fix it before continuing.
