## What this is

A playful daily "oracle card" web app for consciousnessintervention.com.
Visitors see a deck of 69 cards (23 each themed Body / Mind / Spirit) and can
either draw one card for today, or do a Past / Present / Future 3-card
spread. Each card flips over to reveal a short "Light" message and a
"Shadow" (gentle awareness) message. Whichever card(s) someone draws stays
locked in for the rest of that calendar day if they come back.

Inspired by Joe Dispenza, Sadhguru, and Esther Hicks — playful and joyful,
not heavy or fear-based.
